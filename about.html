Our history

Design studio 3D-Style creates harmonious and original interiors, combining a cozy atmosphere and fashion trends. Finished projects are necessarily discussed with the owners of the apartments, if necessary, the desired changes are made to them. This principle of work helps to achieve complete understanding with the customer and create a home to which it will be pleasant to return. Close collaboration of designers with apartment owners helps to create a real fairy tale that is about to come to life in the hands of professionals.

The first consultation defines the concept of all further work. For a short meeting, designers often prove themselves to be excellent psychologists, defining life priorities, character and customer habits. This plays an important role in creating the perfect interior. Experts try to take into account every little thing. Furniture, sanitary ware, finishing materials, accessories - all this is carefully selected and discussed with the client.

Thanks to this responsible approach to the task at hand, the customer can be sure that his wishes will certainly be reflected in the finished project. For clarity, the client even gets acquainted with examples of already implemented projects in order to indicate the design elements he liked or to exclude what is unacceptable for him. This helps eliminate the slightest hint of misunderstanding, so each party can get the most out of cooperation.
