Who we are

We will create the interior of your dreams!

The interior design studio was founded in 2013 by designer Daria Loban and architect Yevgeny Glugovsky. The interplay of our specialties represents the strength of the he.D group's creative partnership. Our experience allows us to recognize the profession with architectural structure and clarity, with design originality and harmony. Always with attention to needs and details, consistent with high quality.

Our mission is simple - to put human needs and desires at the center of everything we do. Together with a multidisciplinary and highly qualified team, he brings his philosophy to life. This means creating a spatial environment in which people feel comfortable; public spaces that make people feel at home; and living spaces that are livable and make sense for the people living in them
